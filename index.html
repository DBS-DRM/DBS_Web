About the Studio

DreamBigStudio was founded on a belief that great architecture and thoughtful interiors change how we feel and live. Every project — from a family home to an urban apartment — begins with careful listening.

Based in Timișoara, Romania, we work across the region bringing precision and warmth to every space we design — pursuing environments that are contemporary yet deeply personal.
